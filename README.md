### History from my understanding.
The sculpture sits outside of CIA HQ, with the ciphertext for all to see. The four sections are all different and so far 3 out of 4 are decrypted. It is important to know that two pieces were available in a museum that assisted in the solving of K1 and K2. Sometime in the 1990s, CIA cryptanalyst and others solved the first two panels using plaintext clues available before their removal. The solution to K3 was introduced in 1999 by a man named Jim. If you would like to know more in detail, visit the CIA [website](https://www.cia.gov/legacy/headquarters/kryptos-sculpture/) or check [Wiki](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kryptos) (fairly reliable for this topic).
